Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What is your lead time for orders?

Most orders are fulfilled within 5-10 business days depending on product availability and your location. Custom configurations may require additional time. Contact us for specific timelines.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes! We offer professional installation services through our network of certified installers across the UK and Ireland. Installation packages include system setup, calibration, and user training. Contact us for pricing and availability in your area.

What warranty is provided?

All our products come with a comprehensive 3-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects and workmanship. We also offer extended warranty options and our premium support packages for enhanced coverage and priority service.

Can I arrange a product demonstration?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive product demonstrations at our showroom locations or can arrange for our technical specialists to visit your facility. Virtual demonstrations are also available for remote customers. Contact us to schedule your personalized demo.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we work with several financing partners to offer flexible payment solutions for our customers. From business financing to lease-to-own options, we can help you acquire the equipment you need with manageable monthly payments. Enquire with our sales team for available options.
